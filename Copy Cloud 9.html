Cloud Nine Event Company

Cloud Nine Event Company is a boutique wedding and event planning firm based in beautiful Lake Tahoe. We know that organizing any large scale event can be overwhelming, especially when you are planning from a distance. In fact, the average destination event requires over 200 hours to organize! Let us simplify the process by connecting you with the area’s best vendors, by providing unique and personal ideas to enhance your big day, by handling the little details so you can focus on enjoying one of the most important moments of your life. With prompt and reliable service, we strive to go above and beyond your expectations to produce an extraordinary Lake Tahoe celebration that is unique and meaningful, one that will remain in your memories for a lifetime.
